Bilingual HR Consulting for Small Businesses

Whether you're in need of a short, mid, or long-term solution, we provide customized and flexible HR solutions to suit your Human Resources needs. We work on or off-site, either as a standalone HR services consultant or in conjunction with your existing human resources team. Current economic trends are now forcing businesses to consider process outsourcing of administrative functions to improve basic services and maintain compliance with the ever changing regulations. Outsourcing daily HR functions, recruitment processes, employee benefit administration and payroll allows key employees to focus on strategic initiatives that benefit the organization, increase productivity and thereby profitability.

HR Outsourcing helps small companies that are in need of a Human Resources Professional on a part time basis relieving the overwhelmed owner or office manager of the daily tasks surrounding recruitment, HR, benefits or payroll. Smart HR Consulting works with management or HR professionals in a variety of different ways. We will work with you to develop an outsourcing model that makes sense for your business and your role in your company taking a bilingual approach when necessary, to meet your employees needs as well.
